Channel & style

Personal plain-text — AJ, reply-to-AJ
Branched email — fires on Pipedrive stage move
LinkedIn DM — manual, ICP-fit only
SMS — optional Day-10 nudge

Voice convention

Brand ribbon — "we" voice from Design Bees
AJ ribbon — first-person, personal, reply-friendly
Designed → Personal → Designed → Personal rhythm is deliberate — the contrast is the premium signal.
How it runs: Trial users pick a plan at signup, so this is a reverse-trial — the sequence reassures and defends, not sells. Day 0 sends from Wix as a branded designed email (welcome + how it works + confirms their chosen plan). Every email from Day 1 onwards fires from Pipedrive, triggered by deal stage moves (Trial Started → Brief Submitted → First Design Delivered → Billing Tomorrow → Converted / Cancelled). Day 1 AJ personal email includes a short Loom recorded per trial; Day 7 and Day 11 are plain-text personal emails. LinkedIn DM on Day 5 is manual and ICP-fit only. SMS on Day 10 is a billing heads-up, not a sales nudge. Day 11 branches on whether they cancelled during trial.
